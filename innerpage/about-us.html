An interdisciplinary approach combining Science, Ecology & Art (the SEA)

At the SEA we believe that:-

"Innovation starts with the needs of your customers and is the process of understanding how people, places, tools and services can be adapted to create the best experience and outcomes for everyone involved".

Understanding this within the wider social, economic and environmental context creates sustainable innovation.

Businesses, now more than ever, need to be in tune with their customers.

Working together with our clients and their customers and partners we help them transform their businesses and provide them with a sustainable future, creating unique and useful brands and services that people tell their friends about.

We have developed projects with Orange, Camper, the DCMS, NEC and the NHS amongst others.
