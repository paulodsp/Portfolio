Ideas come to you in the strangest of places. 2002

We designed, developed and built a digital platform that allows people to share and comment on ideas using text message, email and a simple web interface. The service is a precursor to many of the current web 2.0 favourites like twitter and flickr. The intuitive interface displays a level of simplicity that is rarely seen in current digital design.
